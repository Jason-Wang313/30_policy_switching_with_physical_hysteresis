## Thesis direction

The strongest robotics thesis is that switching robot skills in physical contact is not cost-free: the robot pays a stateful, path-dependent physical hysteresis cost in extra travel, force transients, and re-seating time. A supervisor should therefore optimize over switch cost, not only instantaneous utility.
